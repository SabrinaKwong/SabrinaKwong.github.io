Paint tool

For my "Photoshop" tool, more like paint tool, is one I wanted to add a bit of political commentary on. Particularly a look at gender pronouns in China. In Mandarin, the word for "he", "she", and "them"--All almost sound the same "ta" (keys q, w, e, r). I believe there is not much LGBTQ representation in mainland CHina as people can only identify with male or female. Yet, I also like the play on words and how the pronouns almost sound the same. I have used the preset 1 to 10 to certain brushes that symbolize the color of China (red and green- the colors of gems) as well as "glitter" for being pretty and glamorous and color dots for gender identity (the different colors one identifies with).
